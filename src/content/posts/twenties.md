title: "The lessons I learned from my 20s"
date: "2025-02-05"

Your twenties teach you speed.

How fast people leave.  
How quickly plans collapse.  
How confidence comes and goes.

You chase versions of yourself that never stay long enough.  
And one day, you realise the lesson was never about arriving.

It was about surviving gently.
